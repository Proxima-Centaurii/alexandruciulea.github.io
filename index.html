About me

My name is Vasile-Alexandru Ciulea, I am a 25 years old Matemathics and Computer Science student. I am very interested in computer systems, software design and computer simulations.

From very early on in my life, I was introduced to computers and it wasn't long until I became extremely interested about video games and how they are made. In highschool, I took my first steps towards programming a game engine from scratch in order to understand the underlying algorithms behing every game. Through this experience I was learned various design patterns, data structures, debugging and testing methods, as well as developing a strong interest in areas of software development outside video games. With this project, I also started to truly appreciate and recognise the importance of mathematics in computer science.

The amount of new topics I came across in while working the game engine project and subsequent ones was overwhelming and for this reason I decided that traditional education was more fitting, especially when it came to mathematics. Enrolling into my current course was a great decision since I have a concrete way of measuring my progress, I learn two complementary subjects and I also connected with brilliant, like-minded people who inspire me.
