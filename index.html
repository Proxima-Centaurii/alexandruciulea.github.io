About me

My name is Alexandru Ciulea and I am a Mathematics and Computer Science graduate with a passion for working with data, creating tools and simulators.

The reason for studying Mathematics in addition to Computer Science was that these two disciplines are complementary. Subjects in Mathematics provided a way improve my problem solving and analytical skills while subjects in Computer Science provided me with a solid theoretical foundation and practical applications in web development, embedded systems, and in the study of algorithms and data structures. In addition, studying both subjects gave me the opportunity to connect plenty of brilliant people and work with them on projects.

In my spare time I enjoy solving programming challenges. Most recently, I started taking part in Google's FooBar timed challenges, currently on-going and near completion. Appart from the programming challenges, I also research a wide range of topics in Computer Science such as web technologies, frameworks, embedded systems, programming languages, design patterns and best practices. Part of the research is writing code and starting projects which is done to solidify the knowledge aquired. In the projects section of this page, there is a list of some of my projects that are available on Github.
