About me

My name is Vasile-Alexandru Ciulea, I am a 25 years old Mathematics and Computer Science student. I am very interested in machine learning, software design and computer simulations.

From very early on in my life, I was introduced to computers and I quickly became interested in video games and how they are made. In highschool, I took my first steps towards programming a game engine from scratch in order to understand the underlying algorithms behind games. Through this experience I learned various design patterns, data structures, debugging and testing methods, as well as developing strong interests in areas of software development outside video games. I also started to truly appreciate and recognise the importance of mathematics in computer science.

The amount of new topics I came across while working on the game engine project and subsequent ones was overwhelming. For this reason I decided that a university degree would be beneficial, especially in regards to mathematics. Enrolling into the Mathematics and Computer Science course was a great decision because I have a concrete way of measuring my progress, I learn two complementary subjects and I also get to connect with brilliant, like-minded people.
